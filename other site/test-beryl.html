Mighty Nein Visualization Project!

Last Update: 11/16/2019

This website was created as part of a homework assignment for the University of Minnesota Data Analytics BootCamp. The intention of the assignment was to demonstrate skills in CSS and HTML coding.

We were encouraged to find our own data and create visualizations to present on the website created for the assignment. As an added challenge, I decided to leverage Crit Role Stats taken from the wildly popular Critical Role web series. Thank you to Crit Role Stats for collecting such fun data to play with!

For those unfortunate enough to never have heard of Critical Role - it is a web series where a group of professional voice actors get together and play D&D (a.k.a. Dungeons and Dragons).

For this assignment, all visualizations on the website use stats generated from Critical Role's second campaign up to episode 83.
